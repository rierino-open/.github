### What is Rierino?

Rierino is a low-code microservices development platform designed for scale and flexibility. It enables teams to build high-performance APIs, event-driven flows, and composable digital systems — without writing everything from scratch.

🔧 **Core Platform** — Develop microservices, APIs, and backend logic with a drag-and-drop low-code interface. Deploy on Kubernetes, public/private cloud, or on-premises.

🛒 **Composable Commerce** — Power B2C, B2B, and marketplace models with flexible product management, personalization, and omnichannel experiences.

🤖 **Agentic AI** — Embedded AI agent builder for enterprise automation, with native support for OpenAI, LangChain, and bring-your-own ML models. First low-code platform to support embedded ML since 2020.

📊 **Data & Automation** — Business rules engine, complex event processing, workflow orchestration, and real-time dashboards — all configurable, not coded.
